prioritize.io is a quick way to discover and focus on exactly what matters.

How does prioritize.io work?

1. Create a list

Distill your priorities down to a list of items, talk with your peers to make sure you start with the right list - that the items are the right size.

2. Everyone weighs in

Put your list into prioritize.io, the output is a survey URL you can share. Responses are stored and analyzed to give you a view of what the group thinks is most important, and where the disagreements are.

3. Agree, then Execute.

Armed with an understanding of what your group thinks matters, and exactly where they disagree - get into a room and Discuss. Get out 30 minutes later and Execute.
